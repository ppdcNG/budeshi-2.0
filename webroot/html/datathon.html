BUDESHI DATATHON
In commemoration of the 3rd Anniversary of the launch of Budeshi, we present to you the “Budeshi Datathon”. For 3 years, Budeshi has been highlighting the relevance of contracting data gotten from Ministries, Departments and Agencies to the welfare of the citizens. Budeshi Datathon is intended to be a collaborative and rewarding event comprising of a number of activities/challenges centered around the use of data from our civic tech tool Budeshi to promote and advocate for better service delivery.
Winners will be announced after collation and evaluation of submissions on the 7th September 2018

Writers Challenge
This is an essay challenge/competition in which participants are required to write a blog post or article related to procurement, corruption, transparency and accountability, making reference to budeshi and/or predominantly using data sets from the platform.

Steps

  1. Log on to www.budeshi.ng
  2. Explore procurement data using the features on Budeshi (project search tools and charts).
  3. Come up with a story or blog post related to procurement data sourced from budeshi.
  4. Click here to submit your story.

Criteria for Selection

  1. Articulation (How well the story was pieced together)
  2. Relevance and relativity (How much does it relate to the present issues faced by citizens )
  3. Reference to Budeshi (How well does the story put Budeshi on the spotlight? )
  4. There will be two winners selected with cash prizes of #50,000

Duration

The Writers Challenge is expected to run for 3 weeks from the 20th of August 2018 to 7th September 2018. The challenge will be officially announced on friday the 17th August and participants can then start submission of entries. Entries will close on the 7th of September to enable judges collate and evaluate entries.

Date of Reveal

Winners of the challenge shall be announced on friday the 21st of September 2018 after the judges have evaluated the entries.
